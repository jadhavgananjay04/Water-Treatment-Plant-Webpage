Parameter Information

  • pH

    Normal Range: 6.5-8.5
    If pH < 6.5 or > 8.5: Harmful for aquatic life

  • Turbidity

    Safe Range: < 100 NTU
    If > 100 NTU: Indicates high suspended particles

  • BOD (Biochemical Oxygen Demand)

    Safe Range: < 30 mg/L
    If > 30 mg/L: Indicates high organic pollution

  • COD (Chemical Oxygen Demand)

    Safe Range: < 250 mg/L
    If > 250 mg/L: Indicates chemical contamination

  • Ammonia (NH3)

    Typical Range: 1.5 - 1.8 mg/L;

  • Dissolved Oxygen (DO)

    Desirable: > 5 mg/L in effluent

  • Nutrients

    Aggregate nutrients; Safe range ; 50-150 mg/L
